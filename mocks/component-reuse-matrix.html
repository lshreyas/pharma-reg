Content Architecture · Component Reuse

Documents are assemblies. Facts are the atoms.

Underneath the dossier, a small set of building blocks — a results table, a mechanism paragraph, a dosing statement, a safety narrative — gets re-expressed across many documents. Each row is one building block; each column is a document. A filled cell means the block shows up there. Click a block to watch it fan out across the submission, or a document header to see what it’s assembled from.
